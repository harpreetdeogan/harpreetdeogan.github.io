How

TO APPLY / USE

METHOD-1 :  Line your lips with a matching lip liner. Apply the lipstick directly by pressing the lipstick on your lips with a light even pressure. Press your lips together to even out the color.


METHOD-2 :  Line your lips with a matching lip liner. Use a brush to apply your lipstick, put a small amount of color onto the brush and then fill in your lips.



PRO-TIP:-


Give the illusion of a perfect pucker by applying a lighter shade of lipstick at the center of your lips, on top of a darker shade to add depth and dimension to your lips.
